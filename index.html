About Us

Fuelsave Tech Private Limited is a pioneer company which specialises in the manufacturing of High Emissivity Refractory Coatings with its flagship product name Savefuel. Savefuel is a cutting-edge solution design for application across diverse range of industries including Steel industry ceramic industry glass industry and petro chemical industries.  Our coatings are specially formulated to provide excellent thermal insulation properties, high emissivity, and superior resistance to thermal shock & developed for enhance the performance and efficiency of furnaces, kilns, and other High-temperature applications. Our coatings are suitable for a wide range of materials, including metals, ceramics, and refractory bricks. They can be applied through various methods, such as spraying, brushing, or dipping, to achieve uniform and consistent coverage.
